Zebra

The zebra is one of the largest, strongest and powerful felines in the world second only in size to the Siberian Tiger. They are the largest cats on the African continent and are unique among felines in a number of ways but the biggest difference between zebras and other cats is that they are incredibly sociable animals that live together in family groups known as prides.
